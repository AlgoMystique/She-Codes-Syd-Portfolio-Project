About Me


Hello! I'm Sababa, a passionate Software Engineer and UI/UX Designer with a knack for creating innovative solutions. With a strong foundation in both software development and design principles, I strive to build applications that are not only functional but also user-friendly and visually appealing.


I’ve gained hands-on experience through internships at Pathly, leading development of the NGO’s official website, and She Codes Australia, building full-stack applications with React, JavaScript, and Python/Django. My work spans front-end architecture, state management, UI/UX design, and delivering real-world solutions that make an impact.


Outside of coding, I enjoy spending time with my fur child, Bella — a lively 6-year-old tabby who brightens my day! I also love exploring new food spots, traveling, and reading books.


I’m eager to take on new challenges and contribute to meaningful projects. If you’re looking for a dedicated and enthusiastic front-end developer, I’d love to connect!
